# Antigravity SSR Auditor (v2.0) — Technical Specification Report

## Executive Summary
The **Antigravity SSR Auditor** is a Lead DVV (Data Validation and Verification) auditing platform designed with **Zero-Trust Protocols**. It is engineered to identify "undetectable" failures in NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) Self Study Reports that typically lead to immediate DVV rejections or severe score deductions.

## 核心 Framework: The 4-Dimensional Audit
Unlike traditional link checkers, Antigravity performs a forensic deep-dive into every input across four critical dimensions.

### 1. Arithmetic & Data Gravity (Forensics)
*   **Total Integrity Check**: Full recalculation of extracted tables at the decimal level. Detects manual "rounding fixes" or fabrications that don't match row-by-row summations.
*   **Metric Reconciliation**: Cross-references "Student Numbers" across distinct criteria (e.g., matching Criterion 2.1 Enrollment with Criterion 5.2 Outgoing Progression). Flags **Critical Inconsistencies** that trigger audits.

### 2. The Link Protocol Enforcement
*   **Domain Lockdown**: Enforces NAAC SOP requirements that all evidence must be hosted on the **institutional website domain** (e.g., `.ac.in`, `.edu.in`).
*   **Cloud Storage Prohibitions**: Automatically flags `drive.google.com`, `dropbox.com`, and other third-party cloud hosts as "Non-Compliant."
*   **Accessibility Wall Detection**: Detects login prompts, "Request Access" screens, and dead links that cause instant "Unreachable" failures during peer team visits.

### 3. Visual & Metadata Tampering
*   **Metadata Drift Analysis**: Analyzes PDF internal metadata. If a document claimed for a 2022 event shows a "2026 PDF Creation Date," it is flagged as a **Post-Facto Reconstruction**.
*   **Structural Anomaly Detection**: Identifies "floating" text, font mismatches in scanned certificates, and suspicious whitespace indicating altered dates or names.

### 4. SOP 2025 Compliance Engine
*   **Metric-SOP Alignment**: Maps documentation against the **2025 Standard Operating Procedures**.
*   **Document Type Validation**: Ensures mandatory document types (e.g., "Audit Reports" vs general "Utilization Certificates") are present for specific metrics.

## Technical Specifications & Accuracy

### Supported Inputs
*   **Primary Input**: Multi-page PDF (SSR Documents).
*   **Extraction Engine**: Azure AI Document Intelligence / Production-grade PDF Parse.
*   **Verification Protocols**: 
    *   **Platform Verifiers**: Specialized handlers for Coursera, NPTEL, DOI, Swayam, GitHub, and LinkedIn.
    *   **AI Analysis**: Integrated with **Claude 3.5/4.5 (via Azure AI)** for semantic proof validation.

### Accuracy & Reliability
*   **Deterministic Logic**: Link protocol and arithmetic checks yield **100% precision** on domain and summation failures.
*   **Semantic Verification**: AI-driven analysis provides **95%+ accuracy** in identifying "Private/Login Required" vs "Public" certificates.
*   **Zero-Trust Bias**: The system is biased toward "Suspicious" verdicts when evidence is ambiguous, ensuring institution-side auditors correct issues *before* official submission.
